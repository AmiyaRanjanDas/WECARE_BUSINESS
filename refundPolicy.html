Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Appointment Cancellations

We understand that sometimes you may need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. You can cancel your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charge. To cancel an appointment, please log in to your account and follow the cancellation instructions.

2. Rescheduling Appointments

If you need to reschedule your appointment, you can do so up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charge. To reschedule an appointment, please log in to your account and follow the rescheduling instructions.

3. Late Cancellations

If you cancel your appointment less than 24 hours before the scheduled time, a late cancellation fee may apply. The fee amount will be specified at the time of booking.

4. No-Show Policy

If you do not show up for your scheduled appointment and do not cancel, a no-show fee may apply. The fee amount will be specified at the time of booking.

5. Refund Policy

If you are entitled to a refund, we will process it within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at the time of booking. Please note that it may take additional time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund to your account.

6. Exceptional Circumstances

In exceptional circumstances, such as a medical emergency, please contact our support team as soon as possible. We will review each case individually and strive to provide a fair resolution.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cancellation & Refund Policy, please contact us at support@yourwebsite.com or call our customer service at 1-800-123-4567.
